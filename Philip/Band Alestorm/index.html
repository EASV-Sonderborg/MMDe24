Gareth Murdock

Since joinin' the crew in 2008, Gareth Murdock has kept Alestorm steady on their wild voyages with his deep, thunderous bass lines. His rumblin’ low-end sound is the very keel of the band’s ship, keepin’ everything in balance.

Elliot Vernon

Elliot Vernon be the one who strikes the keys and lets loose the beast within with his thunderous growls. His harsh vocals and melodious keys create a stormy mix of metal madness and pirate mischief.

Máté Bodor

Máté Bodor joined this fearsome crew in 2015, bringin’ his razor-sharp riffs and solos to the frontlines. His guitar be like a cutlass in a pirate’s hand, slicin’ through the stormy waves of metal with deadly precision.

Duckie

Duckie be a symbol of all that Alestorm holds dear: fun, absurdity, and a bit o’ madness. The crew unleashes this colossal rubber duck during their raucous live shows, lettin’ him float above the sea o’ metalheads while the band sings their sea shanties.

Peter Alcorn

Peter Alcorn joined the crew in 2010, and since then, his booming beats have been the wind in Alestorm’s sails. With every mighty blow to the drums, he sends ripples through the sea and keeps the ship sailin’ straight.

Christopher Bowes

As the bold captain of this pirate crew, it’s Cap’n Bowes who steers the ship with his mighty keytar and rum-soaked shanties. His plunderin’ lyrics and rowdy tunes set the course for Alestorm’s adventures on the high seas o’ metal.
