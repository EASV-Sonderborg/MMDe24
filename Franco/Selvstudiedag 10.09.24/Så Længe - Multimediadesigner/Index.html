What can you learn from me?

Sometimes learning with a side-mate is easier than with a teacher, because the language is different, its fresher, and around your level of understanding. Looking at things together with me can provide a different more playful perspective to something way more technical.

The best learning happens when you have someone next to you, walking the same path and sharing the same struggles. A classmate can teach you things a teacher never will, because you're discovering the journey together.
